Training is essential to a dog’s development, setting the foundation for good behavior and strengthening the bond between pet and owner. At Ruffgers Dog University, we offer a range of training programs to cater to dogs at different stages of their learning journey. No matter the training path, our dog boarding facility is committed to helping your dog develop into a well-mannered and happy companion.



A Comfortable and Safe Boarding Experience

When it comes to boarding, we know leaving your pet behind can be challenging. That’s why our boarding facility is designed to provide a home-like environment where dogs can feel safe and relaxed during their stay. Our Residence Hall offers spacious, comfortable accommodations with a structured routine that keeps dogs engaged and at ease.

Unlike traditional kennels, our boarding experience is centered around personalized care. Our dedicated staff ensures that each dog receives attention, exercise, and the routine they are accustomed to at home. With experienced professionals overseeing their well-being at all times, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing your dog is in caring and capable hands. Whether it’s a short stay or a more extended visit, we make sure every dog is treated like family.



Engaging and Structured Dog Daycare

To remain happy and healthy, dogs need physical activity, mental stimulation, and socialization. Our daycare program is designed to provide a structured and enjoyable environment where dogs can engage in safe play, build social skills, and positively expend energy. Whether your dog enjoys high-energy play or prefers a more relaxed setting, we tailor their daycare experience to match their personality and needs.

Our experienced team carefully supervises all interactions, ensuring dogs are grouped appropriately based on size, temperament, and activity level. This attention to detail allows us to create a safe and fun atmosphere where every dog can thrive. By the time they return home, they’re happy, content, well-exercised, and mentally stimulated.
